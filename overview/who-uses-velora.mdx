## Scope of execution

- **Market aggregation.** Best price across every DEX, AMM, and on-chain liquidity venue Velora supports — across one chain or many.
- **Delta intents.** Gasless, signature-based swaps filled by a competitive solver network (Portikus). MEV-protected by construction.
- **Augustus RFQ.** On-chain limit orders and OTC for ERC-20 with optional partial fills.
- **Crosschain.** Single-signature Delta orders that resolve across chains via bridge-aware solvers.

## Heritage

Velora launched in 2020 under the name ParaSwap. The protocol rebranded to Velora in 2025 alongside the migration from PSP to VLR (the network's only token). The execution stack, contract addresses, and core API surface continue uninterrupted — same engine, new name.

## Audited
