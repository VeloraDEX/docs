OTC (over-the-counter, formerly Augustus RFQ) is a direct trade between two known counterparties rather than a swap discovered through a public route. A **maker** signs an EIP-712 order off-chain naming a specific **taker**; only that taker can fill it on-chain through the AugustusRFQ contract — a private, counterparty-specific, fillable-by-one order.

<Note>
  Detailed per-endpoint reference lands with the full OTC OpenAPI spec. Today the surface exposes `POST /rfq/orders` — submit a signed AugustusRFQ order.
</Note>
