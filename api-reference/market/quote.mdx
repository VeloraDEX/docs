---
title: "/quote"
description: "Retrieve a Delta price, with optional fallback to Market via mode=ALL or mode=MARKET."
openapi: "/api-reference/specs/delta.json GET /quote"
---

One call, best execution. With `mode=ALL`, `GET /quote` upgrades a Market swap to a gasless, MEV-protected Delta intent whenever a solver can fill — and otherwise returns a `market` block with the same shape as the [`GET /prices`](/api-reference/market/prices) `priceRoute`, ready for [`POST /transactions/:network`](/api-reference/market/transactions). You never compare routes client-side; `fallbackReason` tells you why a downgrade happened.

<Note>
  This endpoint accepts `mode=DELTA`, `mode=MARKET`, or `mode=ALL`. See [Trading
  modes](/integrate/trading-modes) for guidance and fallback semantics.
</Note>
